❓ Builder FAQ
How do I export my site? ▾
Click "It's Go Time 🚀" in the top right. Your HTML, CSS, and JS files will download automatically. Drop them on Vercel or Netlify and you're live.
How do I save my progress? ▾
Your project autosaves every 30 seconds. You can also manually save from the Projects tab. Use Version History to create restore points before major changes.
How does Push All Changes work? ▾
The "Push All" button in the top nav applies your current Global Styles (colors, fonts, spacing) to all eligible blocks at once. Individual block settings still override global ones unless you push again.
Can I embed third-party widgets? ▾
Yes. Add the "Widget / Embed" block, paste your iframe or script embed code (Elfsight, Tidio, Cal.com, Stripe, etc.), and it renders live in the preview and exports perfectly.
