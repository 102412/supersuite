FAQ

Common Questions

An access code is a short code that grants you entry to the Supersuite builder. During the beta, every plan has a unique code. Enter it on the login screen to access your dashboard. Future versions will support full email & password authentication.
Limits reset daily at midnight (local time). Free users get 1 site export per week and 30 minutes of active builder time per day. Basic: 1 site/day, 60 min. Pro: 3 sites/day, 180 min. Agency: unlimited. The builder tracks session time automatically while you're actively editing.
Clicking "It's Go Time 🚀" downloads three standalone files: index.html, style.css, and script.js. These files include all your edits — colors, content, fonts, block order, animations — and can be uploaded to any web host or opened directly in a browser.
Yes. The "Custom CSS" template in the Templates tab accepts any CSS snippet — including @import for Google Fonts or any framework. Your custom CSS is applied site-wide and included in all exports.
Plan upgrades are available immediately — click the tier badge in the top nav after logging in to open the upgrade panel. Billing is handled via Stripe (coming in SSV26.2). During beta, contact us directly to upgrade.
Yes. Supersuite auto-saves your blocks, styles, and site name to browser session storage every 30 seconds. When you return and log in, your previous work is restored automatically. Full cloud persistence is planned for SSV26.2.
